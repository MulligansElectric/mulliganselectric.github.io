About Us

Customer service is our #1 priority.
Mulligan's Electric Ltd. is located at 762-6th St. Brandon, MB and is owned and operated by Rob Mulligan and Terri Giesbrecht.

Certified Journeyman

Rob has been a certified journeyman electrician with the Inter-provincial Red Seal program since 1995 giving him an array of experience, allowing him to provide expert electrical services and advice to the industrial, commercial and residential fields, as well as, plant maintenance in Brandon, MB, and surrounding areas.

Customer Service

Mulligan's Electric Ltd. is committed to providing highly skilled, professional and reliable services to our clients.
Our company guarantee is that we will put the customer first; providing quality workmanship, and efficient services in a safe environment.

Solar Technology

Our company is currently working to bring Solar Technology to your home and commercial properties. Rob is certified to assess and install solar technology into your home or business. Please contact us for more information.
